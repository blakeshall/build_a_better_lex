Hi!

I'm Blake Hall, a concerned urbanist in Lexington, KY.

Lexington, like most American cities, is overly car-dependent and the majority of the city is unsafe for pedestrians and cyclists alike. Here I plan on pointing out problems that exist throughout the city, critique proposed developments, and offer some potential solutions to make Lexington more livable for all.

Feel free to email me at blake@buildabetterlex.com if you have questions, concerns, or just want to chat.
